About Me

Welcome! My name is Dominique, I’m a 22 year old girl who loves working out just as much as I do eating :). I began my fitness journey in 2019 just trying to work towards a healthier lifestyle and mindset. I fell in love with exercising because of how it made me feel.

Having the power to change my mindset and body is truly amazing, Through working out I realized the only thing I needed to accomplish my goals was myself.

Now I’m here to help you become the best version of yourself- without dedicating hours in the gym or giving up your favorite foods entirely. A healthy lifestyle doesn't need to be a miserable one! And I am so excited to help you grow into your OWN goals while living a healthier lifestyle.
